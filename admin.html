Create New Event

Add a new match night, dual, or tournament. You can attach slots and sign-ups later.

What happens next?

  • The event is saved into your events table in Supabase.
  • It will appear on the public Events page if “Show on public page” is checked.
  • You can attach slots (volunteers, food, etc.) from your Manage Events screen.
